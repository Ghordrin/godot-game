## Thermal

Thermal creates a burning ground patch.

Role:
- persistent area damage
- anti-regeneration
- soft pressure against armored enemies

Thermal should feel like a damage zone, not a burst effect.

## Plasma

Plasma creates a static electricity field.

Role:
- area control
- repeated small shocks
- short micro-stuns

Plasma should feel different from Lightning:
- Lightning chains instantly.
- Plasma controls an area over time.
